Açaí bowls, smoothies & healthy food to go in Fuengirola

The Oasis is your freshness stop on the Costa del Sol: premium açaí bowls topped with fresh fruit, smoothies and cold-pressed juices with no added sugar, and artisan crepes. Everything comes in two sizes (M and L), made to order and packed to go — or build your own bowl, smoothie or crepe with the fresh fruit on display. Perfect for the beach, after the gym or a stroll through town. Find us at Avenida Ramón y Cajal 30, in the centre of Fuengirola.

Frequently asked questions

Where can I get an açaí bowl in Fuengirola?

At The Oasis, Avenida Ramón y Cajal 30 — a short walk from the beach promenade. Bowls come in two sizes (M and L) from €5.50, made to order with fresh fruit, or build your own from €6.90.

Do you have vegan and gluten-free options?

Yes. Bowls, smoothies and juices are naturally gluten-free and can be made fully vegan. Crepes contain wheat flour. Plant milks at no extra cost.

Can I order for pickup?

Yes — message us on WhatsApp at +34 602 38 29 12 and it'll be ready when you arrive. Open Monday to Saturday, 12:00–23:00.
